THE CORSEA ISLANDS

LEADER: TIDE PODS
CURRENT RELATIONS: FRIENDLY / ALLY

DESCRIPTION

The Corsea Islands is an archipelago northwest of the Fellant Archipelago colony. It is a colony of the Enerian Confederation, and is a popular, tropical tourist destination. They have lots of fishing and pearl mining industries, and multiple vehicle manufacturers.

HISTORY

In 1910, after the fall of Eusan Empire, Duke Vineta and the Dutchess voyaged to the Overseas Territories of Corsea, forming the The Empire of Corsea. 28 July,1991, Conservative Party members and 100,000 enraged civillians declared a war on King Corels, which then 4,000 government architectures was burned down or destroyed, King Corels then fled the Country, while most government officials and their family was captured and executed. The Conservatives would then passed the Lancaster Treaty, handing the land to Eneria.

GEOGRAPHIC DATA

The Corsea Islands & Archipelago is located closely to the Fellant Archipelago colony, directly northwest of it. It is also an archipelago, being a group of islands.

THE DIRECTORATE

LEADER: UNKNOWN
CURRENT RELATIONS: HOSTILE / TOTAL EMBARGO

DESCRIPTION

An expansive industrial empire fueled by corporate greed and resource extraction. The Directorate views celestial bodies merely as mining sites and indigenous populations as biological tools for labor.

HISTORY

The Directorate arrived at Oros seeking the rare metals of the Southern Hell. They enslaved the Fellant people for a decade, forcing them into high-pressure mining zones while the Directorate officers hid in pressurized bunkers. This era ended in a total revolution led by Aurelius, resulting in the complete erasure of Directorate presence from the Krios system. We do not know what happened to the Directorate outside of Krios.

GEOGRAPHIC DATA

Originating from a distant star system beyond the Krios Watch. They relied on industrial elevators and orbital defense platforms to maintain control of the Northern Peaks during the occupation. Currently, no Directorate vessels are permitted within Krios airspace.
